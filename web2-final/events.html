private events

Setting

  • Main room is available from 12pm to 12am.
  • Accommodations from 10 to 60 guests.*
  • Partial buy-out of the space.
  • Events in main room are done in a cocktail party format with space and some seating available.
  • All foods served family-style
  • Parties up to 14 guests are also welcome to reserve our private cellar dining room.**

*Parties of 80-100 guests would require a buy-out of the space (pricing available upon request).
**Seated & Structured Dinners and Wine Tastings can only be accommodated in the Cellar Room.
